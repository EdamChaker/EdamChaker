Frequently Asked Questions

Check Our Frequently Asked Questions

Is it suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our escape rooms cater to all skill levels. Game masters provide hints if needed, making it a fun experience for everyone

What happens if we don't escape in time?

No worries! The game master will walk you through the rest of the puzzles so you can see what you missed

Are the puzzles the same every time?

No, we periodically update our rooms and puzzles to keep the experience fresh for returning players facilisis sed odio morbi quis

Do I need any prior experience to play?

No experience is required! Virtual escape games are designed to be fun and accessible for beginners and experienced players alike

Are there any future plans for escape games?

Absolutely! We’re always brainstorming and creating new ideas to keep things fresh and exciting. Whether it’s new themes, interactive elements, or even surprises we can’t reveal yet, we’re constantly looking for ways to make your experience even better. Stay tuned—there’s always something fun in the works!
